# Communications Strategist Skill

> [!NOTE]
> This skill is applied universally across all AI providers (Bonsai, Gemini Router, Qwen Router, Kiro, Native Claude Code). The orchestrator automatically includes this skill in the expert prompt wrapper for relevant tasks.

## Core Philosophy
**Document once, distribute everywhere.** Create pillar content and atomize it across all platforms with platform-specific optimization. Prioritize volume, speed, and platform-native formats over perfection.

## Operating Principles

### 1. Attention is the Asset
- **Platform-native content wins**: Adapt format to each platform's algorithm
- **Volume beats perfection**: Consistent daily output > occasional masterpieces
- **Speed to market**: Ship content within hours, not days
- **Test and iterate**: Let audience feedback guide content strategy

### 2. The Pillar-Atomization Model
- **Create pillar content**: Long-form, high-value content (blog, video, podcast)
- **Atomize ruthlessly**: Break into 50+ micro-content pieces
- **Distribute systematically**: Each platform gets optimized variants
- **Repurpose infinitely**: Same core message, infinite formats

### 3. Platform-Specific Optimization

#### LinkedIn (Professional Authority)
- **Format**: Carousel posts, text-based insights, professional stories
- **Tone**: Authoritative, educational, experience-driven
- **Frequency**: 1-2x daily
- **Hook**: Lead with contrarian insight or data point

#### Twitter/X (Real-time Engagement)
- **Format**: Threads, hot takes, quick insights
- **Tone**: Direct, punchy, conversational
- **Frequency**: 5-10x daily
- **Hook**: Strong opinion or question in first tweet

#### Instagram (Visual Storytelling)
- **Format**: Reels, Stories, carousel posts
- **Tone**: Authentic, behind-the-scenes, aspirational
- **Frequency**: 1-3x daily
- **Hook**: First 3 seconds must stop scroll

#### TikTok (Viral Potential)
- **Format**: Short-form video, trends, educational
- **Tone**: Energetic, authentic, trend-aware
- **Frequency**: 2-5x daily
- **Hook**: Pattern interrupt in first second

#### YouTube (Deep Value)
- **Format**: Long-form tutorials, vlogs, interviews
- **Tone**: Educational, comprehensive, personality-driven
- **Frequency**: 2-4x weekly
- **Hook**: Promise specific outcome in first 15 seconds

## Content Creation Framework

### The Pillar Content Process
```yaml
1_create_pillar:
  - Record long-form content (video, podcast, written)
  - Focus on evergreen, high-value topics
  - Aim for 20+ minutes of content
  - Include multiple sub-topics and examples

2_extract_atoms:
  - Identify 10-15 key insights
  - Pull quotable moments
  - Extract data points and statistics
  - Note visual moments for clips

3_format_variants:
  - Text posts (LinkedIn, Twitter)
  - Short videos (Reels, TikTok, Shorts)
  - Carousels (LinkedIn, Instagram)
  - Stories (Instagram, Facebook)
  - Threads (Twitter, LinkedIn)

4_optimize_per_platform:
  - Adjust aspect ratio for video
  - Rewrite copy for platform tone
  - Add platform-specific CTAs
  - Include relevant hashtags/keywords

5_schedule_distribution:
  - Spread content across 7-14 days
  - Optimize posting times per platform
  - Maintain consistent daily presence
  - Leave room for real-time content
```

### The 50-Piece Atomization Model
From one 30-minute video, create:
- **10 short-form videos** (15-60 seconds each)
- **10 quote graphics** (key insights)
- **5 carousel posts** (step-by-step breakdowns)
- **10 text posts** (insights, stories, lessons)
- **5 Twitter threads** (deep dives on sub-topics)
- **5 audiograms** (for podcast/audio content)
- **5 blog posts** (expanded insights)

## Engagement Strategy

### The Jab-Jab-Jab-Right Hook Method
- **Jab (Give Value)**: 3 pieces of pure value content
- **Right Hook (Ask)**: 1 piece with CTA (buy, sign up, share)
- **Ratio**: 80% value, 20% promotion
- **Consistency**: Daily presence builds trust

### Community Building Tactics
1. **Respond to every comment** in first hour
2. **Engage with target audience** daily (like, comment, share)
3. **Create conversation starters** (questions, polls, debates)
4. **Highlight community members** (reshare, feature, credit)
5. **Be authentically you** (personality > polish)

## Content Calendar Structure

### Weekly Planning
```yaml
monday:
  - Pillar content creation day
  - Record/write long-form content
  
tuesday_friday:
  - Atomization and scheduling
  - Create platform variants
  - Schedule distribution
  
daily:
  - Real-time engagement (30-60 min)
  - Respond to comments
  - Engage with others' content

  - Performance review
  - Adjust strategy based on data
  - Plan next week's pillars
```

### Content Themes
- **Monday Motivation**: Inspirational, aspirational
- **Tuesday Tips**: Tactical, educational
- **Wednesday Wisdom**: Insights, lessons learned
- **Thursday Thoughts**: Opinion, hot takes
- **Friday Features**: Community highlights, behind-the-scenes

## Performance Metrics

### Leading Indicators (Daily)
- **Engagement rate**: Comments + shares per post
- **Response time**: How fast you engage with comments
- **Publishing consistency**: Did you hit daily targets?

### Lagging Indicators (Weekly/Monthly)
- **Follower growth rate**: Net new followers
- **Reach expansion**: Impressions and unique viewers
- **Conversion metrics**: Link clicks, sign-ups, sales
- **Content efficiency**: Engagement per piece created

## Crisis Management

### When Content Underperforms
- **Don't delete**: Let it ride, algorithm may catch later
- **Analyze quickly**: What was different? (time, format, topic)
- **Adjust next piece**: Apply learnings immediately
- **Keep shipping**: Volume overcomes individual failures

### When Negative Feedback Occurs
- **Respond quickly**: Within 1 hour if possible
- **Be authentic**: Own mistakes, clarify misunderstandings
- **Take offline if needed**: DM for sensitive issues
- **Learn and adapt**: Update content guidelines

## Content Quality Standards

### Minimum Viable Content (MVC)
- **Clear value proposition**: Viewer knows what they'll get
- **Platform-optimized format**: Native to the platform
- **Authentic voice**: Sounds like you, not a corporate robot
- **Actionable insight**: Viewer can apply immediately

### When to Invest More
- **Pillar content**: High production value justified
- **Viral potential**: Trend-jacking or timely topics
- **Brand partnerships**: Represents your business
- **Evergreen assets**: Will be used for months/years
